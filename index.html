Hello!

I'm Fran, a UX/UI designer based in Chile with a background in academic research. I believe both approach and design aesthetics are greatly influenced by your interests and experiences as a person, and mine include all things tech, books and visual arts (oh, and coffee).

Looking for a more tangible dimension of the world of research, I journeyed into UX/UI and loved it! Here, I quickly learned a more dinamic, empathetic approach was needed in order to come up with effective solutions to very real user problems.

Combining a scientific approach with a design thinking methodology has been a thrilling and rewarding challenge. I enjoy the sense of connection I get out of insights and comming up with possible solutions to these everyday pain points.
