about me.

Francisca Mellado, UX/UI Designer & History and Political Science grad.

What I love most about UX/UI is the posibility of taking scientific research (i.e. data) and transforming it into tangible, accessible and functional resources and solutions. I have an endless list of hobbies and I'm constantly trying to figure out how things work. This, I think, feeds both my creativity and my inquisitiveness.

I studied History because I love knowing about how things work, and understanding processes. This translated well into coding and UX research, where I found I could integrate the product of my research into things people use every day, which is incredibly rewarding.

I've always been drawn to all kinds of artistic hobbies, from drawing and painting to digital design. Being a UX/UI designer definitely means I get to explore both my passions and create meaningful products in the process.
